I'm a Ph.D. student at the Functional Programming Lab of the University of Nottingham, advised by Nicolai Kraus. +

At the moment I mainly think about higher categorical structures in and for type theory—in particular, semisimplicial types and models of homotopy type theory. More generally, I am interested in the mathematics of the so-called “computational trilogy” spanning (higher) category theory, logical foundations, and computer programs/proofs.

I was previously part of the Knowledge Discovery group at Fraunhofer IAIS, and then of the SMART project at the Computational Logic group of the University of Innsbruck. Prior to that I studied mathematics at the University of Bonn and the Australian National University.
