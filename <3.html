Hello! I'm a Ph.D. student at the Functional Programming Lab of the University of Nottingham, advised by Nicolai Kraus.

I study the mathematics of the so-called “computational trilogy” spanning category theory, logical foundations, and computer programs/proofs. Currently, I mainly think about higher categorical structures in and for type theory—in particular, semisimplicial types, ∞-categories, and models of homotopy type theory.

Previously, I worked in natural language processing with probabilistic models in the Knowledge Discovery group at Fraunhofer IAIS, and then on formalization and tooling for proof assistants under the SMART project at the Computational Logic group of the University of Innsbruck. Prior to that I majored in mathematics at the University of Bonn and the Australian National University.
